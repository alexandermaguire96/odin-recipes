Poppyseed Chicken

Home

This is a recipe that has been a home family love for many years. I was surprised when I entered my adult life that many people have never had a casserol. This is somewhat of one, the whole dish will be make in a casserol dish afterall.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole Rotiseri Chicken
  • 1 can of cream of mushroom or chicken soup
  • 1 small container of sour cream
  • atleast 1 roll of ritz crackers
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • poppyseeds
  • (optional) 2 cups Rice

Instructions

  1. Start by ripping all of the chicken off of your roticeri chicken.
    There was a time when we made the dish using chicken breasts instead but the roticeri chicken ends up having much more flavor in the end. But if you hate the ripping of the chicke, then by all means, cook some chicken.
  2. In a bowl, mix shredded chicken, cream of mushroom and sourcream
  3. Once thoroughly mixed, add to casserol dish
  4. Some members of my family like to add a layer of rice to the bottom of the casserol. It's good either way, up to you.
  5. Crush up the Ritz crackers. You want them to be in semi large chunks so that they stay crispy, so try not to break them too much
  6. In a small pot, melt the butter on low and add the crackers. We want to cook the crackers in butter until crispy and golden
  7. While the crackers are being prepared, preheat the over to 350 degrees F
  8. top ur chicken mixture in the casserol dish with the cooked crackers and garnish with poppyseeds
  9. Bake for 45 minutes
